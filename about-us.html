About of our website



Our website name is "Tik-koe19" which is named after "Covid-19". Because we want to deliver latest covid news as soon as possible to people.


As "Covid-19" becomes a pandemic disease, the information of this disease is becoming more and more essential to people. The aim of our website is to provide these essential information to people worldwide.


For the good user experience, we used two maps for users to easily navigate through the "Covid-19" news regions by regions. The user can click on a region from the map to see the specific news of that region.


The currently availble maps on this website are "Myanmar" map & "World" map. So the availble news on this website are limited to "Covid-19" news of Myanmar and viral "Covid-19" news on the world.
